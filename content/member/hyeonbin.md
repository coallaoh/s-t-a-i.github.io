Hi, I'm Hyeonbin. You can find my personal page [here](https://hbin0701.github.io)! 

I am a research intern at [KAIST STAI Lab](https://s-t-a-i.github.io) and also a final semester M.S. student in KAIST AI, advised by [Minjoon Seo](https://seominjoon.github.io/). Previously, I was an intern at NAVER Healthcare AI and KRAFTON AI where I worked on Co-playble Gaming Agents for [PUBG](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OdtFL1CDDwc).

My research aims to make language models reason more robustly and generalize beyond superficial correlations. I research about learning signals, training objectives, and evaluation settings that encourage models to develop more reliable and transferable reasoning abilities.
