I am a research intern at KAIST, supervised by Seong Joon Oh. I also work closely with Arnas Uselis, a PhD student in STAI.

My research focuses on improving the generalization ability of AI models by combining multiple modalities. To this end, I am interested in understanding how AI models perceive and represent the world. By making these processes more interpretable, I aim to contribute to the development of trustworthy AI systems that can be reliably deployed at scale in real-world applications.
